My Process

How I
approach design

Good design isn't decoration. It's the result of asking the right questions, testing assumptions early, and shipping with conviction.

01

Discover & Define

I start by understanding the actual problem — not the assumed one. User interviews, stakeholder alignment, competitive analysis, and success metrics before touching a wireframe.

02

Explore & Prototype

Divergent ideation across the problem space, rapid low-fidelity prototyping, and quick feedback loops with real users to validate direction before investing in polish.

03

Test & Iterate

Moderated usability testing, rigorous synthesis, and evidence-based iteration. I test with 5-8 participants per round and typically run 2-3 rounds before final design.

04

Deliver & Measure

Pixel-precise handoff, close developer collaboration, and post-launch measurement against metrics defined at the start. Design doesn't stop at handoff — it stops when the goal is met.
